## ğŸ› ï¸ How It Works

### 1. **Asset Origination & Legal Wrapping**
The real-world asset is placed under a legal wrapper (e.g., SPV, trust, legal contract) that defines the relationship between the token and the asset.

### 2. **Token Creation**
A blockchain-based token (usually an ERC-compliant token or NFT) is issued to represent a share or claim on the underlying asset.

### 3. **Compliance Layer**
Access and transfer rights can be coded in. Think of this as programmable KYC/AML using allowlists or zero-knowledge proofs.

### 4. **Custody & Oracles**
In many cases, a custodian or oracle ensures that the asset backing the token is real, updated, and auditable.
